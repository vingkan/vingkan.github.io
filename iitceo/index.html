StartUP IIT

StartUp IIT is a shark tank style pitch competition for entrepreneurs, hackers, creatives, and pretty much anyone that likes talking ideas.

Feel free to pitch ideas from previous hackathon projects, IPRO projects, or your own start up.

Prizes

More than $1000 worth of prizes will be up for grabs in these three categories:

Social Enterprise

Best business plan for a social venture, based on creativity and potential impact.

Best MVP

Best combination of a business plan and successful execution of a clever and informative Minimum Viable Product.

Best Overall

Like no one ever was.

Schedule

Saturday

  • 5:00 PM Doors open, dinner provided. Introductions and all that jazz.
  • 5:30 PM Team formation. Those with ideas may present to recruit teammates. Breakout sessions and Tinderpreneurship will be available to assist in forming new teams. Then go nuts!
  • 8:00 PM Presentation and activity on how to design da real MVP.

Sunday

  • 7:00 AM Doors open, breakfast provided.

  • 1:00 PM Presentations begin.
  • 3:00 PM Awards and closing marks.
